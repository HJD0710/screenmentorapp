1. Introduction

Screen Mentor ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process your personal information in connection with our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using Screen Mentor, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the provisions of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you create an account or subscribe to Screen Mentor, we collect information such as your email address, device identifier, and subscription details.
  • App Usage Data: We collect information about which apps you use on your device, how long you use them, and your daily usage patterns to provide personalized coaching and analytics.
  • Preferences and Settings: We store your mentor selection, customization preferences, notification settings, and other user preferences.
  • Feedback and Support: If you contact our support team, we collect the content of your messages to assist you.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We collect information about your device type, operating system version, device identifiers, and unique mobile advertising IDs.
  • App Performance Data: We collect data about how Screen Mentor performs on your device, including crash reports and diagnostic information.
  • Usage Analytics: We automatically collect information about your interactions with Screen Mentor, including features used, time spent in the app, and user behavior patterns.
  • Location Information: We may collect approximate location information based on IP address, but we do not collect precise GPS location data.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third parties, including app stores (Apple App Store, Google Play Store) and analytics providers. This information helps us understand how users discover and use Screen Mentor.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Providing and Improving the Service

We use your information to:

  • Deliver, maintain, and improve Screen Mentor and its features
  • Provide personalized mentor coaching based on your usage patterns
  • Generate usage analytics and insights for your digital wellbeing
  • Develop new features, products, and services
  • Fix bugs and troubleshoot technical issues

3.2 Communication

  • Send you service-related announcements and updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and customer support requests
  • Send you promotional communications (with your consent)
  • Notify you of changes to our policies or terms

3.3 Safety and Legal Compliance

  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud or other illegal activities
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements
  • Comply with legal obligations and government requests
  • Protect the rights, privacy, safety, and security of our users and the public

3.4 Analytics and Research

We use aggregated and anonymized data to understand user behavior, improve our Service, and conduct research on digital wellbeing trends.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Service Providers

We share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including hosting providers, analytics companies, customer support platforms, and payment processors. These providers are contractually obligated to use your information only as necessary to provide services to us.

We share subscription and account information with Apple and Google as required to process payments and manage your subscription through their respective app stores.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a subpoena, court order, or other legal process. We will make reasonable efforts to notify you of such requests unless prohibited by law.

4.4 Business Transfers

If Screen Mentor is acquired, merged, or sold, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change and any choices you may have regarding your information.

4.5 Aggregated and Anonymized Data

We may share aggregated or anonymized information that cannot reasonably be used to identify you with third parties for marketing, advertising, research, and analytics purposes.

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, secure authentication, and regular security audits.

5.2 Limitations

While we strive to protect your information, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. You use Screen Mentor at your own risk, and you are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

6. Data Retention

6.1 Retention Policy

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. When you delete your account, we will remove or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.

6.2 Backup Data

Your information may remain in backup copies for a limited period but will not be accessible or used by us during that period.

7.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to access, review, and request a copy of the personal information we hold about you. To exercise this right, please contact us at support@screenmentor.app.

7.2 Correction and Deletion

You have the right to correct inaccurate information and request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal limitations. You can update your account information directly in the app or contact us for assistance.

7.3 Opt-Out of Communications

You can opt out of promotional communications by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or adjusting your notification preferences in the app. Please note that you cannot opt out of service-related communications.

7.4 Device-Level Privacy Controls

Depending on your device's operating system, you may have privacy controls available (e.g., App Tracking Transparency on iOS) that allow you to control data collection and sharing for Screen Mentor.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@screenmentor.app. We will verify your identity and respond to your request within 45 days.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@screenmentor.app. We will respond to your request without undue delay.
